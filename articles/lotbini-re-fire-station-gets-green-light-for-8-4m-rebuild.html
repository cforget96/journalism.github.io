Lotbinière fire station gets green light for $8.4M rebuild

By Christopher Forget · Undated

During the July 5 public meeting, the Vaudreuil-Dorion City Council passed a motion to increase the expenditure and borrowing of an additional amount of $8,475,000 for the construction of the new Lotbinière fire station. The city is also expected to receive an additional $4.5 million in funding for the project.

The old fire station was built in 1966, and it was determined by the city of Dorion in 2018 that the best decision would be to erect a new station rather than renovate the old building. Its location means it will also serve the town of Pointe-des-Cascades, an agreement that was made in 2020.
