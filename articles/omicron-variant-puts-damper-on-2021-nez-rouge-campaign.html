Omicron variant puts damper on 2021 Nez Rouge campaign

By CHRISTOPHER FORGET The 1019 Report · Jan 5, 2022

In light of the province's COVID restrictions, Opération Nez Rouge announced in a Dec. 21 press release that operations across the province would be ending early this season. The Vaudreuil-Dorion chapter was set to end its campaign on Dec. 23, while other operations were schedule to run until New Year's Eve.

"Although the sanitary measures applied were deemed more than sufficient by public health, Operation Red Nose would like to support efforts to reduce contact as much as possible in order to slow down the spread of COVID-19," explained a spokesperson for Nez Rouge Vaudreuil-Dorion.

Despite the premature end to the campaign, more than 80 volunteers made nearly 170 trips over 8 evenings.

Nez Rouge will resume next holiday season, though it is too early to tell if the pandemic will continue to complicate the operation.
