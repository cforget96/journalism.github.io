Volunteers needed at four Vaudreuil-Soulanges long-term care facilities

By CHRISTOPHER FORGET · Feb 2, 2022

The CISSS de la Montérégie-Ouest is looking for volunteers for long-term care facilities across its administrative region, four of which are located in Vaudreuil-Soulanges.

"Being a long-term care centre volunteer means offering a warm presence to residents, supporting recreational activities, helping to prevent deconditioning and much more," said a CISSSMO spokesperson.

Volunteers must be 16 years of age or older and be double vaccinated for COVID-19. Protective equipment and training will be provided.

Here is the list of long-term care centres in need of volunteers in the Vaudreuil-Soulanges region:

Centre d'hébergement de Vaudreuil (408 Saint-Charles Ave., Vaudreuil-Dorion)

Centre d'hébergement de Coteau du Lac (341 Chemin du Fleuve, Coteau-du-Lac)

Centre d'hébergement Laurent Bergevin (200 Perrot Blvd., Ile Perrot)

Centre d'hébergement de Rigaud (5 d 'Amour Rd., Rigaud)
