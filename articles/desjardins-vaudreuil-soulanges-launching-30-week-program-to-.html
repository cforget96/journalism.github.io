Desjardins Vaudreuil-Soulanges launching 30-week program to donate to NPOs

By CHRISTOPHER FORGET · Undated

Starting the week of October 18, Desjardins Vaudreuil-Soulanges will donate $2,000 each to two non-profit organizations. The 15-member board of directors will select two organizations every week for the next 30 weeks.

The announcement comes as part of the group's co-op week, which is geared toward socio-economic recovery at the individual and collective levels. $60,000 from the group's Community Development Fund will be directed toward implementing this project.

A video will be posted weekly to www.caissevaudreuilsoulanges.com and the group's Facebook page to unveil the two organizations selected. The goal of the videos will be to showcase the non-profits to the community.
