Christmas Market List

By Christopher Forget · Nov 14, 2021

VAUDREUIL-SOULANGES CHRISTMAS MARKET LIST

With Christmas now 31 days away, a number of seasonal markets will be inviting people to do some of their holiday shopping locally. Here are some of the upcoming Yuletide-themed markets in the Vaudreuil-Soulanges region.

NOVEMBER

Foodies' Market

When: Every Saturday until December 18 (10am to 4pm)

Where: 580 route Cité-des-Jeunes in Saint-Lazare

What: A collection of artisanal products, including St. Elias Greek Olive Oil and Good Mood Soy Candles. Their page on Facebook is full of more great gift ideas!

Résidence Jardins Vaudreuil's Christmas Market

When: November 27 (10am to 4pm)

Where: 333 Querbes Rd. in Vaudreuil-Dorion

What: Visit the residence by Vaudreuil-Dorion's Bicentennial park for Holiday gift ideas! More details to come on their Facebook page.

Coteau-du-Lac 's Christmas Market

When: November 27 (10am to 6pm) & 28 (10am to 4pm)

Where: 2 Georges-Jules-Beaudet Rd. and Pavillon Wilson 4B, Principale Rd. in Coteau-du-Lac

What: This market will include a food truck, horse and buggy rides, and a number of shows throughout the day. More information is available on their Facebook page.

L'Île-Perrot's Christmas Market

When: November 27 & 28 (10am to 4pm)

Where: 150 Perrot Blvd. in Ile-Perrot (Paul-Émile-Lépine Community Centre)

What: Farmers from Vaudreuil-Soulanges and beyond are coming to showcase their locally-sourced products. Activities at the market will be announced soon. Sign up to participate at marchedessaveurs.com

Festival des Couleurs de Rigaud's Christmas Market

When: November 27 (10am to 3pm)

Where: 5 Pagé Rd. in Rigaud (Chartière de Lotbinière Park - Outdoors)

What: Enjoy artisanal and agro-food products in an open-air setting! More information on the Rigaud city website and the event's Facebook page.

DECEMBER

Furley Hudson Christmas Market

When: December 4 (9am to 7pm)

Where: 411B Main Rd. in Hudson

What: The Hudson butchery, bakery and wine bar is hosting a European-style Christmas Market! A bevy of Holiday-season treats from local businesses will be available. More details to come on their Facebook page.

Les Coteaux's Christmas Market

When: December 4 & 5 (11am to 4pm)

Where: 21 Prieur Rd. in Les Coteaux (Gilles-Grenier Building)

What: Come to this year's instalment to find artisanal products and produce from local farmers! The market will also feature shows and various holiday-themed activities.

Saint-Lazare Christmas Market

When: December 4 & 5 (10am to 4pm)

Where: 1301 du Bois Rd. in Saint-Lazare (Saint-Lazare Community Centre)

What: The town will be celebrating the holiday season with the fifth edition of its Christmas market with indoor and outdoor offerings.

Les Cèdres' Christmas Market

When: December 4 (10am to 4pm)

Where: 145 Valade Rd. in Les Cèdres (des Bénévoles Recreation Pavillion)

What: Les Cèdres is welcoming over 30 artisans to offer visitors a wide range of gift ideas. Stay up-to-date by checking out the event on Facebook.

Saint-Zotique's Christmas Market

When: December 12 2021 (10pm à 4pm) Where: 20 des Maîtres Ave. in Saint-Zotique (Orioles School)

What: Visitors will be able to find unique products from 50 local artisans and vendors, such as weaving, glass painting, sculpture, knitting, jewelry making, painting, soap making, sewing, and much more.
