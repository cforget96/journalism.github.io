Desjardins Vaudreuil-Soulanges offering scholarship funds as part of their 2021 co-op week

By CHRISTOPHER FORGET · Undated

Postsecondary students who are members of the Vaudreuil-Soulanges of Desjardins caisse will have the chance at winning one of 50 grants for a total of $60,000. The annual contest, which runs from October 18 to November 30, comes during the bank's co-op week.

This year's theme is Together when it matters most, which "centres on the cooperative model's people-focused approach in a responsible socio-economic recovery, both at the individual and collective levels," as stated in a press release from the bank.

Since the Vaudreuil-Soulanges caisse's formation in 2009, the contest has awarded $980,000 to 750 winners.

Interested candidates can register at GagneTaBourse.ca. The winners will be announced in December 2021.
