Giant bicycle store moves out of Carrefour Vaudreuil

By CHRISTOPHER FORGET The 1019 Report · Jan 5, 2022

After losing the SAQ, IGA and the National Bank earlier this year, yet another tenant is moving out of Carrefour Vaudreuil. Giant Vaudreuil will be leaving the commercial plaza on St. Charles Ave. and merging with the Giant location in Baie D'Urfe.

"The reasons for shutting down the Vaudreuil-Dorion location are many, including a lack of staff, the condition of the shopping centre and how poorly it is managed, and the reduced access to stock over the pandemic," said Giant franchise owner Tim Stocker, who owns both the Vaudreuil-Dorion and Baie D'Urfe locations.

Stocker was not alone in his criticism of Ventrum Inc., the owners of the plaza. In August, The 1019 Report spoke with other tenants of the mall who complained about the lack of communication between tenants and the landowner. Tenants were also frustrated by the company's reluctance to address urgent renovations, including water pipe repairs.

Ventrum said some developments could be on the horizon in 2022, but would not specify what they might be.
