Desjardins Vaudreuil-Soulanges announces student grant recipients

By CHRISTOPHER FORGET · Feb 2, 2022

As part of their annual contest, Desjardins Vaudreuil-Soulanges has awarded 50 grants to postsecondary students, all members at the Caisse, for a total of $60,000. The students and their families were invited to attend the online announcement of their respective grants on January 22.

The initiative, which has awarded $980,000 to 750 winners since 2009, "centres on the cooperative model's people-focused approach in a responsible socio-economic recovery, both at the individual and collective levels," according to a Desjardins press release.

Grant amounts varied between $500 and $5000 depending on whether students are registered in a vocational, college, undergraduate or graduate program. A total of 581 registrations were received this year.
