Ile Perrot 350th anniversary will feature nearly fifty events over six months

By CHRISTOPHER FORGET · May 11, 2022

To celebrate Ile Perrot's 350th anniversary, the four municipalities of Ile Perrot have teamed up with a special committee of community experts to plan 47 events between May and October. Founded in 1672, the seigneury of Ile Perrot is the oldest European settlement in Vaudreuil-Soulanges and, for a 40-year span, was the western frontier of inhabited New France.

The festivities kick off on May 22, when the windmill at Pointe du Moulin, the island's signature landmark, will be in full operation. Completed in 1708, the windmill is one of only two working New France-era windmills in the province. The park completed restoration work in the fall that will allow for the actual milling of flour, the first time it's done since 2012. From 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. visitors will be able to see the windmill produce flour as it did when the island was a French colony.

This symbolic inauguration of the island's 350th anniversary will get the wheel turning for the varied events that will take place over the next six months. Other events in the month of May include Insularis, an evening of multilingual poetry and choir, which will take place at the Notre Dame de Lorette church in Pincourt on May 29.

Other notable activities include evenings of song and dance in parks various parks, free historical bus tours around the island, the inauguration of patrimonial gardens, archaeological events, a kayak tour around Pointe du Moulin, and many more historical and cultural events.

"We're very excited to share the anniversary's programming with the residents," said 350th anniversary president and Ile Perrot historian Lise Chartier. "Thanks to the collaboration of its many partners and volunteers, the Ile Perrot 350th Anniversary Committee is offering opportunities for celebration for all ages and tastes."

The 350th anniversary committee has created a Facebook group, 350e de l'Ile Perrot, to keep residents up-to-date on the schedule of events.
