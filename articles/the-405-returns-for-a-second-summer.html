The 405 returns for a second summer

By CHRISTOPHER FORGET The 1019 Report · May 25, 2022

"The project generated a lot of interest last week at the the Quebec Union of Municipalities, where it won the 'Votre Coup de cœur' award, " said Guy Pilon, Mayor of Vaudreuil-Dorion.

From Wednesday to Saturday, the 405 's evenings will be animated according to four main themes. The park will offer improvisation league performances on Wednesdays, stand-up comedy in collaboration with Just for Laughs on Thurdays, musical festivities including a Beatles tribute, Disco, Country and more on Fridays, and three Saturday outdoor movie nights on July 16, 23, and 30. The city will have bar service on event nights, and a food truck will be on site on Friday nights.

The city is also introducing "Mon premier 405," a call for projects from emerging artists and performers from the region.

"We want citizens to continue to take ownership of the site and to discover all its facets, so we 're giving up-and-coming artists from Vaudreuil-Soulanges, of all ages, the chance to experience the in a different way, by performing on stage. In fact, it 's not too late to register for the Mon premier 405 call for projects, which closes on May 31," says Vincent Bastien, the town's Director of Recreation and Culture.

The park will also offer a variety of workshops to adults during the day, including yoga, landscape watercolor painting, wine glass painting, mixology, and much more. Residents must register for the workshops on the city's website.

The city will continue to offer rental equipment on site, including yoga mats, lawn games, books and more. There is also the park's centerpiece, la Grande Tablée, a round table built around a large tree that can be reserved for meals. The city specified that reservation parties must be 8 people at the minimum. Reservations will begin on June 13 at 9 a.m. for the period of July 4 to August 28 and on August 15 at 9 a.m. for the period of August 29 to September 25.
