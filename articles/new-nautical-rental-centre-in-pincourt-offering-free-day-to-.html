New nautical rental centre in Pincourt offering free day to residents

By CHRISTOPHER FORGET · Undated

The city of Pincourt announced Friday that on July 28, residents will be able to rent kayaks, canoes, paddle boards, and peddle boats, free of charge.

Since its inauguration on June 24, the nautical centre, located in Bellevue park along the water, has been very popular among residents. After a long lockdown, the city wanted to help the community enjoy the beautiful Daoust bay.

The nautical centre is a two-year pilot project, but Pincourt city officials are expecting it to stick around.

"In the future, we'll need to adjust the parking lot to accommodate all the people coming to the park" said Vicky Sauvé, Director of Communications and Citizen Relations.

The special is being offered exclusively to Pincourt residents, and renters will be required to show identification at the kiosk.
