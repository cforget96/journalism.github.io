Vaudreuil-Dorion seeks community feedback for The 405 park

By CHRISTOPHER FORGET · Sep 29, 2021

After a successful first season, the city of Vaudreuil-Dorion has launched a survey for The 405 park, which overlooks the water on Saint Charles avenue. The survey is open to all park visitors, residents or not, until October 7.

"Throughout the summer, The 405 park was a gathering destination that combined activities for all tastes," the city explained in a press release. Now, they'd like to know what they can improve upon for next year's season.

The survey comes as part of the city's 5-year plan, an important part of which includes encouraging community wellness and interaction.

"One of our key projects is to promote a culture of public participation by engaging citizens through consultation and involvement. This is why the city is increasingly consulting its citizens," said director of communications France Lavoie.

The city also plans to consult the public on the future of the Harwood Nature Park in the coming months.
